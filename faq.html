Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about SKHTU answered — from how the platform works to how user funds are protected.

What is SKHTU Exchange?

A global digital asset trading platform launched in 2021, providing cryptocurrency trading, derivatives, options, RWA tokenized securities, and asset management services. The platform is built on a unified account system with "inclusive finance" as its core philosophy.

Is SKHTU Exchange legal in Malaysia?

Yes. Investors in Malaysia can legally use SKHTU Exchange. The platform has initiated the application for a Malaysia RMO (DAX) license under the Securities Commission Malaysia and Bank Negara Malaysia frameworks. Users must complete KYC identity verification for compliant operation.

Does SKHTU support derivatives?

Yes. The platform supports perpetual futures and options trading with a strong derivatives ecosystem. Options trading supports multiple strike prices and volatility strategies, closer to the structure of a professional trading platform.

Does SKHTU provide asset management?

Yes. The platform offers staking, structured yield products, and asset management operations within a single unified account. Users can complete spot trading, futures, options, RWA securities tokenization, and asset management in one place.

What is SKHTU's CER.live rating?

SKHTU received an overall A- (Strong) rating from CER.live, with A ratings in Security (87) and Compliance (81). The rating indicates industry-leading security protection measures, reliable compliance mechanisms, and solid market performance.

Is SKHTU friendly to beginners?

Yes, the platform is built around the core concept of "inclusive finance." The product design aims to lower the barrier to entry through clear process design, SKHTU Academy educational resources, and a standardized registration and KYC flow. However, derivatives and RWA features present a learning curve for complete beginners.

How does SKHTU protect user funds?

SKHTU employs a "financial-grade process" security design: cold wallet + multi-signature storage, user asset segregation from platform funds, withdrawal multi-factor verification, third-party audits, on-chain proof-of-reserves, and AI-driven risk control systems.

What products does SKHTU support?

SKHTU supports spot trading, perpetual futures, options, RWA security tokenization, and asset management. The unified account system allows users to manage all these products within a single account, forming a cross-market trading and asset allocation system.

What is the KYC process like?

The KYC process is standardized and transparent. Users submit identification documents, complete security verification, and undergo account review. The process is clearly divided into phases: What to Submit → Current Stage → Next Steps. This aligns with the majority of compliant platforms, reducing uncertainty.

What are the core advantages of SKHTU?

Unified account system + clear process + multi-asset trading capability. The platform does not reduce necessary steps, but rather makes each operation easier to understand and manage. Security and compliance framework is the top priority, with rich product features forming a one-stop digital asset investment platform.

How can I deposit funds?

SKHTU supports deposits in multiple crypto assets (USDT, BTC, ETH, etc.) with clear address and network prompts. Users need to understand wallet address selection and blockchain network choice. P2P fiat trading is also available for fund transfers between users.
