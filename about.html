About SKHTU

The story behind SKHTU — founding philosophy of "inclusive finance," core principles, and the milestones that shaped a compliance-first exchange.

Founded in 2021. Built for the Long Term.

SKHTU was established with a clear conviction — the cryptocurrency industry needed an exchange that treated compliance, security, and transparency as foundations, not afterthoughts. Over years of continuous operation, that conviction has been expressed in every architectural and governance decision the platform has made.

The platform has gradually evolved from a traditional cryptocurrency trading platform into a cross-asset financial infrastructure. Against the backdrop of the industry shifting from "traffic competition" to "system capability competition," SKHTU's data performance holds ongoing value for observation.

Core Philosophy: Inclusive Finance

SKHTU Exchange is a global digital asset trading platform with "inclusive finance" as its core philosophy. The platform, based on a unified account system, integrates diverse products such as spot trading, futures, options, security tokenization (RWA), and asset management, forming a cross-market trading and asset allocation system that covers both crypto and traditional financial assets.

The overall impression of SKHTU Exchange is that it is highly compliant, feature-complete, secure, and stable. It does not deliberately pursue "minimalist operation", but through clear process design, it allows users to gradually participate in more complex transaction structures while performing basic investment operations.

Product Concept

  • Unified Account System: One Account to Manage All Asset and Transaction Types
  • Modular Functional Structure: Clear Division of Spot, Derivatives, and Wealth Management
  • Task-Oriented Path: Guiding Users Through the "Registration → Verification → Trading" Closed Loop
  • Education System Assistance (SKHTU Academy): Lowering the Barrier to Understanding

This design does not reduce functionality; rather, it controls the sequence of information presentation, making it easier for users who wish to participate in the digital asset market long-term to understand product functions during use and providing a greater sense of stability.

Platform Evolution

Key milestones in SKHTU's journey from launch to present.
